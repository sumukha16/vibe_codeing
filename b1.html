Bugatti Chiron

Specifications

  • Engine: 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16
  • Horsespower: 1,479 hp
  • Torque: 1,180 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • 0-60 mph: Approximately 2.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 261 mph (electronically limited)
  • Price: Starting at around $3 million

Features

  • Carbon fiber body construction
  • Adaptive suspension for a comfortable and sporty ride
  • Advanced aerodynamics with an active rear wing
  • Luxurious interior with premium leather and customizable options
  • State-of-the-art infotainment system with bespoke sound system
